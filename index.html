About Toñita's Restaurant

Toñita's Restaurant is a Honduran Restaurant that serves authince Honduran food. We serve Honduran food from the costal area of Honduras, our most popular dish is Pollo Con Tajadas and it is served with fried green banana, is mainly served in the costal area. The national dish of Honduras is the Baleadas, which is a big flour tortilla filled with refried red beans and Honduren crema and Honduran hard cheese. You can also put scrammbeled eggs or steak meat or even grilled chicken or best of all you can put all of it inside the Baleada. We make our flour tortillas by hand everyday and you can taste the difference of home-made tortillas from store-bought tortillas. Our seafood soup is made with coconut milk and it comes with a varity of seafood including shrimp and crap legs.
