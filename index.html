Toñita's Restaurant

Toñita's Restaurant is a Honduran Restaurant that serves authince Honduran food. We serve Honduran food from the costal area of Honduras, our most popular dish is Pollo Con Tajadas and it is served with fried green banana. The national dish of Honduras is the Baleadas, which is a big flour tortilla filled with refried red beans and Honduren crema and Honduran hard cheese. You can also put scrammbeled eggs or grilled steak or even grilled chicken or best of all you can put all of it inside the Baleada. We make our flour tortillas by hand everyday and you can taste the difference of home-made tortillas from store-bought tortillas. Our seafood soup is made with coconut milk and it comes with a varity of seafood including shrimp and crap legs.


Toñita's Restaurant

Toñita's Restaurant es un Restaurant 100% catracho que serve comida de la costa de Honduras. Servimos Pollo Con Tajadas,Tacos Dorados, y las Baleadas. Las Baleadas son con tortilla hechas a mano todos los dias y viene con frijoles y mantiqua con queso raydo. tambien se le puede echar carne y huevo picado. El caldo de marisco es hecho con leche de coco y viene con camarones y patas de cangrejo y piscado y muslo con verduras y arroz.
